Why This is Important

Everyone deserves to have a sleek, fashion-forward, and camouflage way to be able to listen to music. With a wide range of skin tones, and further ones being made every day with your feedback, everyone will be able to have their own way to listen to music. Instead of having a bright white Airpod in your ear, have a sleek way to listen to music however and whenever you want to.

In order to recieve more feedback about the tones of the U-Airpods, we have started out with simple designs. These are the basic U-Airpods, but we have also made it possible to recieve custom U-Airpods that match your skin tone perfectly. In order to do this online, you can look up any foundation that you know fits your skin color, and then enter the brand, name, and ID code provided, to be able to create your own specific range. In stores, just go to any Apple store and ask to be scanned for your own specific skin tone in order to perfectlty create your own
